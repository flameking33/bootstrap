Cat facts

  • cats are the most popular pet in the USA, with 88million cats kept as pets.
  • Cats have over 30 muscles that control their ears.
  • Cats sleep 70% of their lives, and in some pretty crazy positions.
  • A cat can rotate its ears 180 degrees.
  • Unlike dogs, cats do not have a sweet tooth.

Dog facts

  • There are more than 150 dog breeds, divided into 8 classes: sporting, hound, working, terrier, toy, non-sporting, herding, and miscellaneous.
  • Dogs are the most popular pet on the planet, with a third of all households around the world having a dog.
  • Dogs evolved from a now-extinct species of wolf.
  • The "smell" center of a dog's brain is 40 times larger than that of humans.
  • Dogs dreamlike people.
